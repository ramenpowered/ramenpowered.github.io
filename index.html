  • Welcome!

    Countryside Kennels is located in the north-east corner of Port Coquitlam. The property also contains the home of the owner, Cathy Isherwood, so there is always someone on the property through the night.

Boarding

Here at Countryside Kennels, we offer a more traditional approach to dog boarding. One thing that is offered is daily outside, individual strolls to allow the dogs to relieve themselves outside of their kennels. Additionally, regular walks allow the dogs something to look forward to.

Training

We offer basic obedience training as well as behaviour modification. Cathy has been training dogs for almost 20 years and has worked with dogs of all breeds. Though basic obedience is the main focus of our traning, Cathy has also worked with people to solve individual issues.

Grooming

Countryside Kennels offers grooming to boarding clients as well as to other dogs in the vicinity. From a simple nail clip, bath and dry, or to full scissor cuts, Countryside Kennels can assist you in all your pet's grooming needs. For prices, please visit our grooming page.
