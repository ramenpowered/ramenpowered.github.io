Training

Countryside Kennels offers basic obedience training as well as some behaviour modification. Cathy has been training dogs for almost 20 years and has worked with dogs of all breeds and sizes. Though basic obedience is the main focus of Countryside Kennels training, Cathy has also worked with people to problem solve individual issues.

We offer group obedience lessons as well as private lessons.

Private lessons are $40/hour and group lessons are $100 for eight, one hour lessons. In-kennel training is also available. This is where the dog stays with us and is trained during their stay.
