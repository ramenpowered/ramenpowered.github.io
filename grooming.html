Grooming

Countryside Kennels offers grooming to boarding clients as well as to other dogs in the vicinity. From a simple nail clip, bath and dry, or full scissor cuts, we can assist you with your grooming needs.

Rates

Grooming prices start at $35.00 for a small dog and vary from there with the breed of dog, condition of the dog’s coat and the haircut desired.

Price includes: ear cleaning, nail clipping, bathing and fluff drying as well as complete hair cut. For an estimate on your dog’s grooming needs, give us a call!
